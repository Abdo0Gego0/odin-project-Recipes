Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g pecorino cheese, grated
  • 50g parmesan, grated
  • 2 cloves of garlic, peeled
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • A small bunch of fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to the packet instructions.
  2. Meanwhile, fry the pancetta with the garlic until crispy. Discard the garlic.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the pecorino and parmesan.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and quickly toss with the pancetta.
  5. Off the heat, mix the egg mixture into the pasta, stirring quickly so the eggs don't scramble.
  6. Season with plenty of black pepper and sprinkle with parsley before serving.
