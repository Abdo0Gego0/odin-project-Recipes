Beef Tacos

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix
  • 12 taco shells
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 onion, finely chopped
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning mix and a little water to the beef, simmer until the sauce thickens.
  3. Warm the taco shells according to package instructions.
  4. Assemble tacos by filling each shell with beef, then topping with lettuce, cheese, tomato, onion, sour cream, and salsa.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve immediately.
