Classic Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • Maple syrup for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour batter onto the pan, about 1/4 cup for each pancake
  4. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with maple syrup and butter.
