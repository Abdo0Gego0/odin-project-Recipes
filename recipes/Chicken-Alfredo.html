Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 200g fettuccine
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine in salted water according to package directions.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook in a skillet over medium heat until done. Remove and slice.
  3. In the same skillet, melt butter and saut√© garlic for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Slowly stir in the parmesan until smooth.
  5. Toss the pasta in the sauce, add the chicken, and stir to coat everything well.
  6. Garnish with parsley and extra parmesan before serving.
